Security

We understand the importance of keeping your customers safe and comfortable. If you choose us, you will have many means of doing so. We have planned for every eventuality and strive to go the extra mile to prevent any hiccup, big or small, from occuring. You can relax whilst your festival runs as smoothly as possible for both you and your attendees.

  • SIA and non-SIA Security Guards
  • Dedicated search areas positioned at festival entry sites.
  • State of the art wristbands, with near impossible-to-replicate features to combat counterfeiting.
  • Watch towers and trained staff to oversee areas, ensuring that anything that happens will be responded to as quickly as possible.
  • Staff who are equipped to deal with fires.
  • On-site police presence and make-shift headquarters.
  • On-site medical staff, and a 'safe-space' tent for attendees who need time to recover.

Catering

We are partnered with a huge variety of independent food vendors, ensuring that there is something even for the pickiest of eaters! There are even vendors who in special diet requirements, such as gluten-free and vegetarian/vegan.

We provide options for both bar volunteers and paid bar staff, or even a mixture of both. We have the equipment and facilities to flexibly set up bars around the available sites in a place of your choosing.

In addition to food and drink, we also have options for selling general festival items, such as wellies and ponchos, smoking paraphernalia and band/festival merchandise. Please contact us for further details, or if there is something else you'd like to dispense at your festival.

Hosting

We have several different sites of differing size across the country. This allows you to pick one that matches the scale of your festival, and ensures you have your festival at a location of your choosing. We understand the complexity of organising an event like this, and have endeavoured to provide you with the facilities to put your mind at ease.

  • Multiple sites to choose from.
  • We can provide stages and speakers, and the means to deliver and set them up.
  • Information staff and kiosks
  • Volunteer litter picking and recycling facilities
  • Portaloos and waste management
  • Assigned space for car parking on-site
  • Admin staff
